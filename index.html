07 / Process

How I Work

A simple, transparent 4-step process that ensures every project ships on time, on budget, and beyond expectations.

01
🔍

Discovery & Planning

We start with a detailed conversation about your goals, audience, and brand. I map out the site structure, define scope, agree on timelines, and document every requirement before writing a single line of code. No surprises — ever.

02
🎨

Design & Prototyping

Whether you bring Figma files or need me to design from scratch, I create pixel-perfect layouts reviewed and approved before development begins. Typography, spacing, color systems, and component states are all locked in at this stage.

03
💻

Development & Testing

Clean, semantic HTML. Efficient, maintainable CSS. Vanilla JavaScript with no bloat. Every build goes through cross-browser testing, mobile responsiveness checks, Lighthouse audits, and accessibility reviews before you see it.

04
🚀

Launch & Support

Deployment is handled with care — optimized assets, proper meta tags, sitemap, and canonical URLs. After launch, you get 30 days of free support to catch any edge cases and ensure everything runs perfectly in the real world.
